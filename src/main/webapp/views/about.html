Welcome to the About Us page!

A little history lesson...

We are Freddy's Files, your one-stop online e-shop that suits all your gaming needs. We sell all types of games ranging from First Person shooters all the way back to the classics like Pong. But here's the kicker: we don't just sell digital copies, we also sell the cases and disks so you can have that authentic gaming experience.

So where did we come from and how did we get here? Freddy's Files started as a mess of friends in Seattle, fooling around after class, trying to make web-sites without reading the manual. The group had varied taste in games—RPG, FPS, open-world, puzzles—so the site they made together bears the toolmarks of several styles. When they had enough games, they started selling online. They made friends with the dudes at Kinkos to print up flyers. They burned some CDs to sell. The website got bigger. Of necessity, Freddy’s Files' four members (Frederick Lee, Steven Ballard, Calvin Sithideth, and Cole Hamilton) figured out how to run a small business. GitHub became the construction yard; Microsoft Teams became our virtual garage.

A decade and countless sales later, it’s all properly official—Freddy's Files is now a real, live business with international distribution—but not that much has changed. Freddy's Files still partners with people who aren’t jerks. If we can’t find something we need, we make it together. Although each member has a career as a solo programmer, every so often the whole crew convenes to make a collaborative project as a group. This collaboration was made by old friends who’ve tuned their craft, both together and individually, for over a decade. And it shows.
